05

Objections, answered

Q

Will free token sets undercut pack sales?

No. Only the oldest set graduates, so every recent set stays premium and keeps its full launch revenue. The graduation reaches the patient earner who was never going to pay full launch price.

Q

Won't paying players just wait for the free version?

The token path still takes months. Impatient players keep buying packs to skip the wait, and levelling still costs money: level 2 requires three copies of a set.

Q

Does this devalue the monthly pop up packs?

No. The pop up packs bundle far more than the bare set, and they stay exactly as they are. Only a single shelved set, already years past its launch, moves into the affordable lane.

Framed softly: this is about keeping mid tier players engaged and closing a gap that grows on its own, not about anyone holding too much.

Recommendation

Adopt a one in, one graduates rule for the Decor Set Token store: at every new launch, the oldest shelved set joins the store. Zero build cost, low risk, and an illustrative +$47,676 a year from the older backlog alone, with newest sets and pop up packs untouched.
